Our Story

About DFS

Founded in 2022 by a collective of creatives, Doha Fashion Show has become Qatar's premier platform where fashion speaks the languages of expression, unity, and exchange. Across our editions, we've welcomed over seventy designers from Qatar, the Arab world, Europe, and Asia — each season a curated experience blending global craft with local voice.
